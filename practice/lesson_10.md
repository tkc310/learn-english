## Lesson09 否定文と疑問文の復習

### 1. 否定文と疑問文にする

1-1. `Naoya studies history.`
- 否定文: `He dosen't study history.`
- 疑問文: `Dose he study history?`

1-2. `Mika and Tom are baseball fans.`
- 否定文: `They aren't baseball fans.`
- 疑問文: `Are they baseball fans.`

1-3. `Sayaka has some pets in her room.`
- 否定文: `She dosen't have any pets in her room.`
- 疑問文: `Dose She have any pets in her room?`

### 2. ()の動詞を適切な形にする  

2-1. `My mom (be) not 50 years old.`
`My mom is not 50 years old.`
2-2. `Dose she (dry) dishes in the morning?`
`Dose she dry dishes in the morning?`
2-3. `You and I (be) in the same course.`
`You and I are in the same course.`
2-4. `She (collect) your luggage.`
`She collects your luggage.`
2-5. `He dosen't (tell) any stories to me.`
`He dosen't tell any stories to me.`
