# Lesson12

## Lesson11の復習

### 1. 英文を選択

1. 男は子供のように見える
`A man looks like a child.`
2. 犬は私たちの友達だ
`Dogs are our friends.`

### 2. 冠詞を選ぶ

1. `I have [ a ] car. [ the ] car is really nice.`
2. `Her [ 無冠詞 ] friend teaches me English words.` -> 所有格なので不要
3. `[ An ] hour has 3,600 seconds.`
4. `You see [ the ] moon at night.`
5. `That [ the ] mountain looks great in November.` -> 支持格なので不要
6. `Don't close [ the ] door, please.`
