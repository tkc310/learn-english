# Lesson12

## Lesson11の復習

### 1. 英文を選択

1. 男は子供のように見える
`A man looks like a child.`
2. 犬は私たちの友達だ
`Dogs are our friends.`

### 2. 冠詞を選ぶ

1. `I have [ a ] car. [ the ] car is really nice.`
2. `Her [ 無冠詞 ] friend teaches me English words.` -> 所有格なので不要
3. `[ An ] hour has 3,600 seconds.`
4. `You see [ the ] moon at night.`
5. `That [ the ] mountain looks great in November.` -> 支持格なので不要
6. `Don't close [ the ] door, please.`

### 3. 不可算名詞を選択

rain money food tennis Australia milk

### 4. 並び替える

1. They ( hours / study / Japanese / a / four) day.
`They study Japanese four hours a day.`
2. also / the / rises / sun / .
`The sun also rises.` -> alsoは副詞のため一般動詞の前

## Lesson12の演習

### 1. 現在進行形 or 過去進行形に直す

1. `Some countries produce new stars.` 現在進行形
`Some countries are producing new stars.`
2. `Does he collect maps and stamps?` 過去進行形
`Was he collecting maps and stamps.`
3. `Her father doesn't hunt birds in the forest.` 過去進行形
`Her father wasn't hunting birds in the forest.`
4. `The professionals think about an international society.` 現在進行形
`The professinals are thinking about an international society.`

### 2. 並び替える

1. (in / are / the / they / swimming / pool / .)
`They are swimming in the pool.`
2. (her / night / sister / was / last / crying / .)
`Her sister was crying last night.`
3. (is / the / not / smoking / man / .)
`The man is not smoking.`

### 3. ing形にする

- enjoy: enjoying
- get: getting
- run: running
- have: having
- study: studying


## Communication Stage 2

1. 君はカナダで日本語を勉強したの？ (study / in Canada / did / Japanese / you / ?)
`Did you study Japanese in Canada?`
2. 君は日本語を話す？ (speak / you / Japanese / do / ?)
`Do you speak Japanese?`
3. いいえ、話しません (don't / no / I / .)
`No, I don't.`
