# Lesson17

## Lesson16の復習

### 1. 前置詞を入れる

1. 私の父は毎日２時に事務所に入る  
`My father enters his office [ at ] 2 every day.`
2. あなたは明日までここに滞在するべき  
`You should stay here [ until ] tomorrow.`
3. その人気店はこのフロアーにある  
`The popular shop is [ on ] this floor.` -> 階の場合は on を利用する
4. その店は郵便局と消防署の間にある  
`The store is [ between ] the post office [ and ] the fire department.`


### 2. 共通の前置詞を入れる

- 1. on
  - `Your glasses are () your head` -> 〜の上に
  - `I have to study the subject () May 2.` -> 特定日
- 2. for
  - `She will stay in London () six weeks.` -> 具体的な期間
  - `I wll buy something () you.` -> 〜のために
- 3. by
  - `My cat likes to sleep () the chair.` -> 〜のそばで
  - `He didn't go to Osaka () train.` -> 交通手段


### 3. 並び替える

1. movies / like / she / her father / loves / .  
`She loves movies like her father. (彼女は父と同じように映画が大好きだ)` -> like〜 で「〜のように」
2. will / during / Hawaii / I / in / this summer holiday / stay / .  
`I will stay in Hawaii during this summer holiday. (私は今期の夏休みの間、ハワイに滞在する予定だ)`
