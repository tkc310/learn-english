## 否定文と疑問文

1-1. `Naoya studies history.`
- 否定文: `He dosen't study history.`
- 疑問文: `Dose he study history?`

1-2. `Mika and Tom are baseball fans.`
- 否定文: `They aren't baseball fans.`
- 疑問文: `Are they baseball fans.`

1-3. `Sayaka has some pets in her room.`
- 否定文: `She dosen't have any pets in her room.`
- 疑問文: `Dose She have any pets in her room?`
