# Lesson14

## Lesson13の復習

### 1. 英文を完成させる

1. 彼は７月に新しいスポーツを始めるつもりはない
`He [ isn't ] [ going ] [ to ] [ begin ] a new sport in July.`
2. 明日、雪は降らないだろう  
`[ It ] [ won't ] snow [ tomorrow ].`
3. 来年、彼女は20歳になる  
`She [ will ] [ be ] 20 yeas old [ next ] [ year ].`
4. 今夜、あなたは出かけるつもりですか？
`[ Are ] you [ going ] [ to ] go out [ tonight ]?`

### 2. 単語を並び替える

1. a school / next / to  / will / year / graduate / from / you / ?  
`Will you graduate from a school next year?`
2. this jacket / going / tomorrow / are / will / wear / you / to / ?  
`Are you going to wear this jacket tomorrow?`

### 3. 指定した数の単語の英文にする  

1. 彼は家を買わないだろう 5  
`He won't buy a house.`
2. 私は映画を見るつもりだ 6    
`I'm going to watch[see] the[a] movie.`

## Lesson14の演習

### 1. 助動詞を入れる

1. 彼は早く起きるべきだ  
`He [ should ] get up early.`
2. 私は4時頃に帰宅しなければならない  
`I [ must ] get home at about 4 o'clock.`
3. ここで昼食を食べれる  
`You [ can ] have lunch here.`
4. 私が彼女にメールを送ります  
`I [ will ] send an email to her.`
5. 彼女は幸せではないかもしれない  
`She [ may ] not be happy.`

### 2. 指定形式に書き直す

1. `Must he do this job on Thursday?` (過去の疑問文)
`Did he have to do this job on Thursday?` -> 過去形は ~ had to だが、過去の疑問文は Did ~ have to
2. `She can raise her children` (willを使う)  
`She will be able to raise her children.` -> raise は育てるという意味もある
3. `Tom has to express his feelings.` (否定形)  
`Tom doesn't have to express his feelings. (トムは感情を表現する必要がない)`
4. `My brother caches a taxi on his way to the station.` (〜するべきだ)  
`My brother should catch a taxi on his way the station.`
5. `I want to have dinner with you.` (丁寧な表現)  
`I would like to have dinner with you.` -> would like ~ で丁寧な言い方になる

### 3. 正しい組み合わせを選ぶ

- Can I ~
- Will you ~
- Shall I ~
- Sall we ~

(ウ) 許可、依頼(意志の確認)、申し出、提案
