# Lesson13

## Lesson12の復習

### 1. 現在進行形 or 過去進行形にする

1. `Bob doesn't watch a movie.`  
`Bob isn't watching a movie.`
2. `Did this fax machine work last night?`  
`Was this fax machine working last night?`
3. `They didn't dance then.`  
`They weren't dancing then.`
4. `Does your sister worry about her son?`  
`Is your sister worrying about her son?`

### 2. 適切な単語を入れる

1. 彼は英語を勉強していた  
`He [ was ] [ studying ] English.`
2. 私は京都に住んでいる  
`I [ live ] [ in ] Kyoto.` -> 状況動詞のため進行形(be動詞 + ing)にしない
3. 彼らは私を知っている？  
`[ Do ] they [ know ] about me?` -> 状態動詞のため進行形(be動詞 + ing)にしない
4. 彼女はテレビを見ていない  
`She [ isn't ] [ watching ] TV.`

### 3. 訂正する

1. `Are you understanding me, Satoshi?`  
`Do you understand me, satoshi?`
3. `Your father weren't working yesterday.`  
`Your father wasn't working yesterday.`
4. `Is hiroko cook fish in the kitchen now?`  
`Is hiroko cooking fish in the kichen now?`

## Lesson13の演習

### 1. 指定の単語数で未来の表現に変える

1. `His cousin doesn't build a house.` (8)  
`His cousin isn't going to build a house.`
2. `She offers some advice.` (5)  
`She will offer some advice.`

### 2. be going to にする

1. `Will you go to Europe?`  
`Are you going to go to Europe?`
2. `The popular writer won't write a new essay.`  
`The popular writer isn't going to write a new essay.`

### 3. will にする

1. `Helen is going to talk to you next time.`  
`Helen will talk to you next time.`
2. `Are they going to invite the teacher at the end of February?`  
`Will they invite the teacher at the end of February?`

### 4. 適切な英文を選ぶ

1. 今夜は雨が降る (強い根拠)  
(b) `It's going to rain tonight.`
2. 私は明日、家にいる予定ではない (単なる予定)  
(b) `I won't be home tomorrow.`
3. 私は明日レポートを提出するつもり (意志を示す)  
(a) `I'm going to hand in a report tomorrow.` -> `hand in ~` で 「〜を提出する」
4. 彼女は学校の先生にならないだろう (客観的意見)  
(b) `She won't be a teacher at school.`
