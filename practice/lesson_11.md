# Lesson11

## Lesson10の復習

### 1. 正しい単語を入れる

1. 彼は先週の日曜部、彼女に電話した？
`[ Did ] he [ call ] her [ last ] Sunday?`
2. 東京できのう、雨は降らなかった
`It [ didn't ] [ rain ] in Tokyo [ yesterday ].`
3. 彼らは全く英語を話さない
`They [ don't ] [ speak ] English at all.`
4. 1年前、私たちはロンドンにいた
`A year [ ago ]. we [ were ] in London.`
5. 彼女は彼に手紙を書きますか？
`[ Dose ] she [ write ] a letter [ to ] him?`
6. 彼女はその時、彼のガールフレンドではなかった
`She [ wasn't ] [ his ] girlfriend [ at ] [ thet ] time.`  
-> 過去形の否定文だが一般同士でないためdidn'tではなくbe動詞のwasn't

### 2. 誤りを訂正

1. `You don't mix a salad then.` don't -> didn't
2. `Were your mother in his house yesterday?` Were -> Was
3. `You and I play the guitar well last week.` play -> played
4. `Did he drove a car at last?` drove -> drive

## Lesson11の演習

### 1. 正しい冠詞を入れる

1. 私はウサギを飼っていない
`I don't have [ a ] rabbit.`
2. 私は牛乳が欲しい
`I want [ 無冠詞 ] milk.`
3. その冗談はあまり良くない
`[ the ] joke is not good.`
4. 私は京都に住んでいる
`I live in [ 無冠詞 ] Kyoto.`
5. その男の子達はとても有名です
`[ The ] boys are very famous.`
6. 私には意見が必要だ
`I need [ an ] opinion.`

### 2. 並び替え

1. is / old / this / an / machine / .
`This is an old machine.`
2. window / please / the / open / , / .
`Open the window, please.`

### 3. 適切な冠詞を選択

1. `There was (無冠詞 water in the glass.`
2. `He is (無冠詞) my brother.`
3. `Is there (an) old tree in your garden.`
4. `She doesn't play (the) piano.`
5. `We had (無冠詞) dinner in his house.`
